## Upgrade instructions
Switch your build to use Gradle 9.3.0 RC1 by updating your wrapper:
```
./gradlew wrapper --gradle-version=9.3.0-rc-1 && ./gradlew wrapper
```
See the Gradle [9.x upgrade guide](https://docs.gradle.org/9.3.0-rc-1/userguide/upgrading_version_9.html) to learn about deprecations, breaking changes and other considerations when upgrading.

For Java, Groovy, Kotlin and Android compatibility, see the [full compatibility notes](https://docs.gradle.org/9.3.0-rc-1/userguide/compatibility.html).
